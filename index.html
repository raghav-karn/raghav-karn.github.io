Learn more about me!

Ayy, I am Raghav Karn, a 15 y/o high schooler hailing from the capital city Patna of Bihar, India. Welcome to my portfolio page! Today, I stand as a self-taught frontend developer, a tech enthusiast, and a aspirant for JEE exam. Might as well add the sugar of a learner to the coffee of my life.

I started to learn coding back in 2020, during the COVID lockdown. I had learned a lot of things since then, with a mind of a programmer and math student — a view of logic. I dropped my coding skills for 2 years after the lockdowns lifted. I resumed this hobby of mine in 2024, after I got to know about Hack Club from GitHub Universe.

I think of myself as a learner, one that must always stay curious and learn new things with each opportnuity. Even if no material rewards may be granted, a developed skillset never goes to waste in life. I will again be dropping off from coding and other things this year, in order to prepare the next two years for my JEE. With that said, I hope my portfolio page is enjoyed by all!
